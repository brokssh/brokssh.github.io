# Privacy Policy

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how your game collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the app. The app does not collect any personal data directly. It integrates Google AdMob for advertising, which is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect
- **No Personal Data Collected by the App**: The app itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as names, emails, device IDs, or location data.
- **AdMob Data**: Ads served via Google AdMob may collect certain information as described in Google's Privacy Policy, including advertising-related data like IP addresses or device identifiers for ad personalization and measurement. This policy does not apply to AdMob's practices.

## How Information Is Used
- The app uses AdMob solely for displaying advertisements to support development.
- No user data is shared with third parties beyond what AdMob requires for ad serving.

## Sharing Your Information
- Information from AdMob is handled according to Google's policies and is not shared by the app developer.
- We do not sell or share personal data with other companies.

## Your Privacy Controls
- Manage AdMob-related privacy through your Google Account settings or device ad preferences.
- For children: The app complies with COPPA; no personal data is collected from users under 13.
